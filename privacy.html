Privacy Policy

ShareLinx is designed to help you save and organize links. We've written this policy in plain language to explain what information ShareLinx works with, where it's stored, and the choices you have. In short: ShareLinx is built to keep your saved links on your own device and under your control.

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Information We Collect

ShareLinx does not require you to create a ShareLinx account, and we do not operate our own user account system. Core use of the app does not require signing up or signing in to ShareLinx.

The app stores the content you choose to save, which may include:

  • The web addresses (URLs) you save
  • Titles associated with those links
  • Notes you add
  • Collections you create to organize your links
  • Link metadata, such as descriptions or images
  • Optional summaries, when that feature is supported and used

This information is stored on your device. ShareLinx does not collect this content into its own servers.

How We Use Information

The information you save is used only to provide the app's features: saving your links, organizing them into Collections, displaying titles and details, storing your notes, and helping you revisit your saved pages. We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use your saved links for advertising.

iCloud Sync

If iCloud is enabled for ShareLinx, your ShareLinx data may sync across your devices through Apple iCloud using your Apple account. This syncing is handled by Apple's iCloud service and is subject to Apple's terms and privacy practices. You can manage iCloud availability for ShareLinx through your device's Apple/iCloud settings.

AI Summaries

When supported and available on your device, ShareLinx can generate optional summaries of saved pages. These summaries are created on your device using Apple's Foundation Models and Apple Intelligence. This feature is optional, and you can use ShareLinx fully without it.

Link Metadata

To display useful details about your saved links, ShareLinx may fetch information from the webpages you save. This can include titles, images, descriptions, or readable text, which may also be used to generate on-device summaries. This metadata is used to provide app functionality and to help you recognize and revisit your saved links.

Data Sharing

ShareLinx does not sell your personal data, and we do not use your saved links for advertising. Widgets and share extensions associated with ShareLinx may access your saved app data only to provide app functionality, such as letting you save a new link or view existing ones. If you use iCloud sync, your data is handled by Apple as described above.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your saved links and Collections remain stored until you remove them. You can delete saved links and Collections directly in the app at any time. If iCloud sync is enabled, deletions may also be reflected across your synced devices through Apple iCloud.

Children's Privacy

ShareLinx is a general-purpose link-saving tool and is not directed to children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Because the app does not require an account and stores data on your device, ShareLinx does not gather personal details beyond the link content you choose to save.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
