Contact & Support

Need a hand with ShareLinx? We're glad to help. Whether something isn't working as expected or you just have a question, reach out and we'll do our best to sort it out.

You can email us anytime at:

support@sharelinx.app

Reporting a Problem

To help us understand what's going on, please include as much of the following as you can:

  • Device model (for example, iPhone, iPad, or Apple Vision Pro)
  • iOS / iPadOS / visionOS version
  • ShareLinx app version or build, if you know it
  • What happened
  • What you expected to happen
  • Screenshots, if you think they'd help

The more detail you can share, the easier it is for us to pinpoint the issue.

Quick Troubleshooting

Before reaching out, here are a few quick things that often help:

  • Restart the app. Close ShareLinx fully and reopen it.
  • Check iCloud. If you're expecting your links to sync, make sure you're signed in to iCloud, iCloud Drive is enabled, and ShareLinx is allowed to use iCloud in Settings.
  • Confirm the page is accessible. If a title, image, or summary doesn't appear, check that the original webpage loads normally.
  • Try saving the link again. A fresh save can clear up occasional hiccups.

A Note on AI Summaries

Optional AI summaries depend on Apple Intelligence support on your device and on having enough readable text on the page. If a summary doesn't appear or looks limited, the page may not have had enough readable content, or your device may not support this feature.
