Privacy Policy

Effective: June 1, 2026

ShareLinx is built on a simple principle: your links belong to you. This policy explains plainly what we do — and don't do — with your data.

What ShareLinx Does

ShareLinx lets you save, organize, and sync URLs across your Apple devices. Links you save are stored in iCloud and synced privately to your other devices using your Apple ID.

Data We Collect

ShareLinx collects only what is necessary to provide the service:

  • URLs and link metadata (title, preview image) that you choose to save
  • Tags or organizational data you create within the app

This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is not accessible to us.

iCloud & CloudKit

Your data is synced using Apple's CloudKit framework. Apple's privacy practices govern how iCloud data is stored and protected. We have no access to your iCloud data. You can review Apple's privacy policy at apple.com/privacy.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect personal information such as your name or email address
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not serve advertisements
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties
  • We do not have access to the links or content you save

Your Data, Your Control

Because your data lives in your iCloud account, you are always in control. You can delete any saved links within the app, or remove all ShareLinx data by deleting the app and clearing its iCloud data in your device's iCloud settings.

Children's Privacy

ShareLinx does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The app is not directed at children.

Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes materially, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of ShareLinx after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact
