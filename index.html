What ShareLinx does
01

Save from anywhere

When you find something worth keeping, use the share sheet in Safari or any app to send it straight to ShareLinx. No copying, no pasting, no switching back and forth.

02

Organize with Collections

Group your links into Collections that make sense to you — "Weeknight Dinners," "Project Research," "Things to Buy Later." A single link can live in more than one Collection, so you never have to choose just one place for it.

03

Add your own notes

Capture why a link mattered. Jot down a quick thought, a reminder, or the detail you'll want when you return to it, so your saved pages stay meaningful instead of mysterious.

04

Browse the way you like

Open ShareLinx on iPhone for quick saves on the go, spread out on iPad for easy organizing, or use Apple Vision Pro–compatible iPad mode for a more spacious view of everything you've collected.

05

Optional on-device AI summaries

On supported Apple Intelligence devices, ShareLinx can generate summaries right on your device, giving you the gist of a saved page at a glance. It's entirely optional and processed locally.

06

Sync with iCloud

Use iCloud sync, if enabled, to keep your links in step across your Apple devices. Your saved links remain yours, and syncing is handled through your Apple account.

Get started

Install ShareLinx, save your first link from the share sheet, and sort it into a Collection. No account needed to begin. Want a closer walkthrough? Visit How to Use for step-by-step guidance.
